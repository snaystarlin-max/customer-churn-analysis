# Customer Churn & Retention Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes customer behavior and service usage data to identify key drivers of churn and uncover actionable strategies to improve customer retention.

---

## Dataset

The analysis uses the Telco Customer Churn dataset, which includes:

- Approximately 7,000 customers  
- 21 features including demographics, services, and billing information  

---

## Key Insights
- Customers on month-to-month contracts have the highest churn rates  
- Early-stage customers are significantly more likely to churn  
- Higher monthly charges are associated with increased churn  
- Long-term customers show strong retention and lower churn risk

---

## Tools Used
- Python  
- Pandas  
- Matplotlib  
- Seaborn  
---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

- Improve customer onboarding experience to reduce early churn  
- Encourage long-term contracts through incentives  
- Provide better value for high-priced plans  
- Identify and target high-risk customers early

## Conclusion
This analysis highlights that churn is driven by a combination of early disengagement, pricing sensitivity, and lack of long-term commitment.

By leveraging data-driven insights, businesses can proactively improve retention strategies and enhance customer lifetime value.
